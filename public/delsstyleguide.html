Welcome to Vitals!

This app allows all your medical records to be in one place. You personally pick and choose which records you'd like to keep track of (ex. bloodwork, Dentist, prescriptions, etc.). Vitals allows you to never have to rummage through piles of records and notes from your doctor(s) again.

Keep a log of all your doctors appointments!

Create your own records you want to keep track of (Dentist, General Practioner, Cardiologist, etc.)

Keep track of all your prescriptions and bloodwork!

You can upload blood test results and keep track of your prescriptions and when to fill them

Record your weight and height and calculate your BMI!

Be able to simply click a button that tells you your current BMI on your profile page
